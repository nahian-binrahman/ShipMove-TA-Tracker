## üöÄ Getting Started
When you log in, you will see the **Tactical Dashboard**. This is your command center.

### 1. Tactical Dashboard (Home)
*   **Today's Missions**: See how many movements are happening today.
*   **Pending Review**: See how many movements need approval from a commander.
*   **Monthly Spend**: Track how much allowance has been used this month.
*   **Quick Links**: Use the "Tactical Launchpad" to quickly start a new movement or search for personnel.

## üë• Managing Personnel (Soldiers)
Go to the **Personnel Registry** to manage the people in your unit.
*   **Search**: Find anyone by their name or Service Number (SN).
*   **Add New**: (Admin only) Click "Add Soldier" to put someone new into the system.
*   **Details**: Click on a person to see their rank and unit information.

## üó∫Ô∏è Tracking Movements
This is the core of the app. You can log where people are going and why.
*   **New Movement**: Click "New Movement" and fill in the form (Who is moving, from where, to where, and when).
*   **TA Calculation**: The app automatically calculates the allowance based on the movement details.
*   **Movement Log**: View a full list of all past and planned movements. You can filter them by date or status.

---

## üõ°Ô∏è Authorization (Review Queue)
If you are a Commander or Admin, you must check the **Authorization Queue**.
*   **Pending Requests**: Look at movements waiting for approval.
*   **Approve**: Click the green "Approve" button to authorize the movement and payment.
*   **Reject**: Click the red "Reject" button if the movement is not correct. You must provide a reason.
